I Pay You

== Description ==

'I Pay You' allows visitors to pay in a very simple way by sending money via PaypalMe, whether it's to make a donation or simply to pay a debt.

How to connect to the PaypalMe account?

The webmaster simply has to insert their PaypalMe ID in the 'I Pay You' management page to create the link to their PaypalMe account.

How to send money?

The visitor simply has to enter the amount in euros in a form and click on the validation button so that the payment link is generated. Clicking on the link will take them to the payment page.

== Installation ==

=== From within WordPress ===

1. Visit 'Plugins > Add New'
2. Search for 'I Pay You'
3. Activate 'I Pay You' from your Plugins page.

=== Manually ===

1. Upload the 'I Pay You' folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the 'I Pay You' plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to "after activation" below.
